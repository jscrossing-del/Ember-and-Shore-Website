About the Practice

Grounded, warm,
and evidence-based care

Ember & Shore Therapy, PLLC is a Minnesota-based outpatient mental health practice founded by Lea Semler, LMFT. We provide relationship-centered, trauma-informed therapy for individuals, couples, and families navigating anxiety, relational stress, life transitions, trauma, and emotional burnout.

As an LMFT, our approach is uniquely systems-based — meaning we look at how the relationships and environments around you shape your experience. Healing rarely happens in isolation, and we hold that truth in every session.

Ember & Shore provides a highly personalized, warm experience for clients who prioritize the therapeutic relationship and a grounded environment.

Who We Help

You may be in the right place if…

You feel emotionally exhausted

Burnout, chronic stress, or the sense of "just getting through the day" — we help you find rest and restore your capacity.

Your relationships feel strained

Couples and families experiencing conflict, disconnection, or communication breakdowns can rebuild trust and closeness.

You're navigating a major transition

Life changes — loss, divorce, illness, parenthood, career — can destabilize even the most grounded person.

You're "fine" but something is off

High-functioning anxiety, quiet depression, or a persistent emptiness — you deserve support even when you appear okay.

You're a child, teen, or young adult

We work with children, adolescents, and young adults, supporting them through anxiety, identity, family stress, and the challenges of growing up.

You've experienced trauma

Every session is trauma-informed. You set the pace. Safety and trust come first, always.

Fees & Insurance

Making care accessible

We believe quality mental health care should be within reach. We offer multiple ways to make sessions work for your situation.

Insurance

We accept Aetna, Carelon Behavioral Health, Cigna, and Quest Behavioral Health. Contact us to verify your specific plan before your first session.

Sliding Scale

Prefer not to use insurance, or uninsured? We offer a sliding scale fee based on household income. Reach out to discuss what works.

Payment Methods

We accept cash, check, Visa, Mastercard, and Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA/HSA). Payment is collected at the time of service.

Have questions about your specific plan or cost? Contact us — we're happy to help before you commit.

Meet Your Therapist

Lea Semler, LMFT

Lea Semler is the founder of Ember & Shore Therapy, PLLC. She believes therapy is a space where we can tend to the "embers" of our past while finding a steady "shore" to stand on today. With almost two decades of clinical experience, Lea has dedicated her career to supporting individuals, couples, and families through their most complex transitions.

Lea earned her Master's in Marriage and Family Therapy from St. Cloud State University and has been a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist (LMFT) since 2008. Her background includes experience in residential treatment, education, and private practice. She specializes in Anxiety, Trauma (TF-CBT trained), Mood Management, and Relationship Dynamics.

Outside the office, Lea is a lover of all things warm — from summer cabin days with her husband and children to the peace of the ocean and a winter fireplace. She believes in the healing power of good food, a solid sense of humor, and a steady path forward.
